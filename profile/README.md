## 🚀 Overview
HWMonitor for Windows is a free, lightweight hardware monitoring software that reads your PC's built-in sensors and lays out the results in one tidy window. It reports CPU temp, GPU temp, drive temperatures, voltages, fan speeds, and power draw, and it logs the minimum and maximum of each so you can catch spikes as they happen. Its simple, uncluttered interface makes it easy to confirm your components are running cool and stable — handy for troubleshooting, testing a new build, or just keeping an eye on things. A single HWMonitor download gives you a reliable snapshot of your hardware's health whenever you need it.

## ✨ Features
- **Temperature Tracking** — Monitor CPU, GPU, motherboard, and hard drive temperatures live.
- **Voltage & Fan Data** — Read system voltages, fan speeds, and power consumption in real time.
- **Min/Max Recording** — Review the lowest and highest values logged during your session.
- **Wide Sensor Support** — Recognizes a broad range of chips, mainboards, and components.
- **Small & Straightforward** — A compact interface that shows the numbers that matter, clearly.
- **Portable Version** — Run a portable build without installing whenever that suits you better.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open HWMonitor — it detects your hardware and lists each component's sensors automatically.
2. Expand a device to see its temperatures, voltages, and fan speeds.
3. Watch the Min and Max columns to understand how the readings shift while you use your PC.

5. Leave the window running in the background to keep tabs on your hardware over time.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is HWMonitor free?**
Yes. HWMonitor is free to download and use for monitoring your hardware. There's also a paid HWMonitor Pro version with additional features for those who want more.

**Is HWMonitor safe?**
Yes. HWMonitor is a legitimate, read-only monitoring tool — it reports the values from your PC's sensors without changing any settings. To be certain you get the genuine software, download it from the official source.

**What extra features does HWMonitor Pro add?**
HWMonitor Pro is a paid upgrade over the free version. It adds capabilities such as graphs, remote monitoring, and a customizable interface for users who need deeper insight into their hardware.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
HWMonitor works on modern versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping Windows and your drivers up to date helps it read your sensors accurately.
